How this game works:

In the following lines the basic mechanics of Escape The Island are being explained.

Your character

There is an icon of your character on the left side of your item bar. Try clicking on it.

Action points

After selecting your character, you will start with a fixed amount of action points. Several actions your character can perform will reduce your action points, like collecting items or creating new items. The more action points you have left when completing the game, the better. Your best character will appear in the statistics.

Navigation

There are different locations on the island. If another location is available where your mouse pointer is located , an arrow appears. Click on this arrow to enter the new location.

Collecting items

On the island you will find different things which can be more or less useful. You can collect them if you click on an item. The item now will be located in your item bar.

Interacting with objects

There are several objects that you can interact with. Just click on them.

Using items on objects

You also can use an item to perform an action on an object. For example you could try to use a bottle on a waterfall to fill the bottle with water. All you have to do is to select the item in your itembar and click on an object. You can only use one item on an object at the same time.

Create new items

To proceed with your escape plans, you will have to create new items out of the ones you possess. First, select several items in your itembar, then use the tool symbol in form of a hammer to create a new item if that is possible.

Interact with NPCs

Even if you think you are alone on this island, you will soon find out that you aren't. Click on an NPC to interact with him. He may give you quests, items, useful pieces of information, or teach you something helpful.

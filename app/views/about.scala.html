About the game

Escape The Island is a 2D point-and-click-adventure. Assuming the role of a stranded civilian, you have to think on your feet to lead his situation to a success.

Our mission

We created a 2D point-and-click-adventure which is called “Escape The Island”. The user controls the main character being stranded on an island and has to help him to escape.

The user can...

  • move between the different locations of the island

  • interact with objects

  • gather and use items

  • interact with NPCs (non-playable character)

  • combine different objects and items

  • receive an evaluation on his success

Furthermore there are different endings, so stay curious.. ;)
